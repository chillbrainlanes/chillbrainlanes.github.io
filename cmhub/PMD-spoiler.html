Pokémon Mystery Dungeon Commander Legends is a Commander draft set that adapts the world and characters of the game series. Partner up with every playable Pokémon from the main series, or take on the role of an iconic character, in fast-paced, combat-heavy, free-for-all Commander action!

The set's draft format is played with standard Commander Legends draft rules, with the exception of Color Identity, which is not used during deck construction. Players have access to up to two copies of Hero of Endless Possibility to use as Commanders, in case they failed to draft any suitable ones.
