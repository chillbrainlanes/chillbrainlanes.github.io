Viridian's Last Mission is a set inspired by super spies, super villains, action movies, and a touch of cosmic horror. When a mad billionaire mind controls the keeper of an ancient lineage of druids into releasing the Nightmares of the plane, Justiciar's number one agent has to come out of retirement to face this new threat, alongside a paranormal expert who's in over his head.

Viridian's Last Mission was in Revolution Custom Standard from September 2021 to September 2023.
