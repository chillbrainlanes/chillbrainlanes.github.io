Episode 25: Finale

Well, our dream of hearing Megalovania by the end of this game did not come true, but No More Nuzzles is so much better than I could've possibly dreamed. What a perfect track for this final battle against a guy named Varik.

Boy, I got lucky with full paras in this battle. I also made sure to get some Wrap action in for this fight: It really helped against Marbitar, although I'll admit it maybe wasn't strictly necessary against Floratress. Still it was quite poetic that our two oldest team members combined their powers to take down Varik's ace, right? Especialy with using Wrap, the thing this series is named after. And you all get to watch every single Wrap in real time, just like I did during the playthrough, how awesome is that.

Varik brings out the waterworks over his parents, as is typical. We get to see an accidentally placed extra space character, we enter our Beasts into the Hall of Fame and we're played out to Cha La Head Cha La. Folks, that's Star Beasts!

You can look forward to at least one bonus episode containing my full review-style thoughts on the game, but for now: Thank you so much for reading if you've gotten this far, I hope it's been as fun for you as it's been for me. And if you're reading this the day it releases, I hope you have a very Merry Christmas.

See you!
