happy birthday to me (not really)

one year site anniversary!

today's the one year birthday of this site! the first post says it was written the day before, but the site didn't actually go up till today. i'd wager 99% of all traffic to this site has come from the past week, with folks checking out shandalar 2003. and if you're reading this, there's a good chance you're coming from there too. thank you for all the positive feedback on the game, by the way! i'm working hard to issue balance and bug fixes to make the game the best it can be.

as for what's next for this site, i've alluded to it a couple of times but there's a big magic related post that i wanted to have done around christmas of last year but it's been a very big project and i've decided to put it off until the end of this year instead. so please look forward to that! otherwise i'd like to write more posts on here than i did in the past year, but i can't make any promises on that front just yet. either way, i'll keep posting brainwaves and whatever else i feel like on the site for y'all to enjoy. thanks for taking the time to read this, and for checking out the site in general! while you're here, make sure you sign the guestbook!
