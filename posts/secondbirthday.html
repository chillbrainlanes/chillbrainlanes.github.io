happy birthday to me again (still not really)

two year site anniversary!

it's the belated two-year anniversary of the website! sorry for missing it on the date this time, lol. it's been a big year for chillbrain lanes. shandalar 2003 gained a lot of fans (shoutouts to all the folks still playing it and sending me emails about it, i love getting those!), the giant magic crossword was a blast to put together, the classic christmas song throwdown was a lot of fun, and more recently the site now hosts chillbrain radio (including the gasterclass podcast) and the new state route 52 section, which i plan on updating regularly. my vision for the site originally was for it to hold all kinds of little links and pages to explore, and it's really starting to get fleshed out into that which is awesome.

thanks to everyone who's checked out the site in the past, and thanks to you for reading this message! look forward to more exciting stuff here in the future!
