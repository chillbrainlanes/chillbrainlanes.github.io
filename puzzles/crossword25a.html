Welcome to the Chillbrain Lanes Magic: The Gathering Crossword 2025!

Last year's crossword was just something I made for fun on a whim, but a lot of folks really seemed to enjoy it. Or rather, they seemed to enjoy seeing one person in particular struggle through it. That stream was an absolute delight, and in it Mr. Wheeler requested that I make another puzzle for the next year. I was happy to oblige!

Like the Serebii crosswords that inspired it, the original crossword wasn't really meant to be something anyone could realistically complete without a lot of outside help. It was just a fun "How many do you know?" type of challenge. However, Mr. Wheeler asked for this year's puzzle to exclude particular types of questions: Nothing about any card that isn't Vintage legal, and no questions about ancillary story and lore. With that in mind: Theoretically, if you had 100% perfect knowledge of every printing of every Vintage-legal card, and knew where all of them came from, you would be able to easily answer every single question on this puzzle. Certain clues give some extra information that's outside of these criteria, but none of it is necessary to solve that clue. It's just some extra details that might help.

This puzzle clocks in at a whopping 400 Clues, 150 more than last year. Out of these, 99 clues come from submissions from friends and community members. Thank you to everyone who submitted, by the way! The full credits can be found at the bottom of this page.

Here's some quick ground rules to keep in mind before you jump in to the puzzle:
• All answers are made up of only letters. No numbers, no hyphens, no punctuation or any other symbols. Case also doesn't matter, naturally.
• When the answer would include one or more spaces, or a character that isn't a letter, write the answer without them. If there's an answer with an unconventional version of a letter, use the version of that letter found on a common English keyboard. For example "Lim-Dûl's Vault" would be "LIMDULSVAULT" as an answer.
• An answer can be anything, not just a card name. However, all answers are in English.
• That thing about the "pay attention to the capitalization in the questions!" from last year was stupid, so we're not doing that this time. If you don't know what I'm talking about, good.
• If you get stuck on something, the "Solve" button will fill in any answer for you. "OK" is the button you'll want to press if you're filling in a question yourself. The "Check puzzle" button will tell you which if any of your currently filled in answers are incorrect.
• Unfortunately, there is no way to save your progress across sessions, so if you want to pick up a partially completed puzzle later, you'll have to leave the tab open. Apologies for the inconvenience!

With that out of the way, grab a drink, turn on some (festive perhaps?) music, and maybe go use the restroom, cause you might be here for awhile. When you're ready, click below to venture into the dungeon. Good luck!
