State Route 52

The Midwestern Road Trip Card Game RPG

Tarn

Tarn has lived his life on the road for nearly as long as he can remember. As a self-proclaimed "Adventurer", he travels the world in search of new people to meet, places to see, and treasure to collect. While he's traveled alone for his entire adult life, he establishes an "Adventuring Party" with his friend Mela after she is forced to leave her home. Kitt and the Player both wind up joining this group, although Tarn is initially hesitant on having to "babysit a couple of kids." (Both Kitt and the Player are in their late teens).

He often comes off as aloof and sarcastic, but he has a tender side that's only seen in private with close friends— In public, he tries to carefully cultivate a worldly, carefree image. They'd never say it to his face, but most people who meet Tarn for the first time come away thinking he's a bit of an asshole, so this might not be the best idea.

Tarn has a passion for food and cooking, and takes great pride in preparing meals for the party using whatever ingredients they were able to forage. Each morning, he can always be found awake bright and early preparing breakfast and a fresh pot of coffee over the fire— He loves black coffee, although nobody else in the party has quite acquired a taste for it yet.
